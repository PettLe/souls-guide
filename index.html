From Software's Soulsborne series has become a synonym with crueling difficulty. While these games are (most of the time) fair and their reputation has been HUGELY exaggerated, no doubt they still are hard as nails. Especially in the beginning! These games don't hold your hand or tell you about the crucial mechanics which would make your journey that much easier. So here is a small guide for each of the games, telling you the very basic tips and tricks for a smoother start! This guide isn't for people who want a step-by-step guide through the early game but for those who wish to actually figure out the game on their own, while finding it a little bit overwhelming or intimidating!

Don't give up, Skeleton!

Dark Souls

Demon's Souls was maybe the game that started it all, but this is the game that made the genre widely known.

General tips:

You will die a lot. But don't let that discourage you. It is part of the learning experience.

  • It's not always bad to be hollow. While it does prevent you from summoning help, it also prevents invasions!
